ATLAS

An expedition and field-research studio. Pilot a continuous photoreal camera flight through four vast natural worlds by scrolling. Cinematic, grounded, real.

01 — Dunes

Endless dunes under the first gold. The desert breathes in the low light. Long shadows mark the passage of wind across time. This is where every expedition begins.

02 — Canyon

Light carves stone in hidden silence. A slot canyon holds the day in fractured beams. Layer upon layer, the rock remembers every season.

03 — Coast

Where the storm meets the ancient shore. Sea stacks stand defiant against the Atlantic. Spray rises, light falters, and the horizon dissolves into night.

04 — Summit

Above the clouds, the stars take the watch. At the roof of the world, the last ember of day yields to the first cold stars. The expedition reaches its quiet summit.
