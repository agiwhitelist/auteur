ABYSS

A deep-ocean research collective. Pilot a continuous photoreal descent from the sunlit surface to the abyssal floor by scrolling — five zones of the sea, each darker and stranger than the last.

0m — The Surface

Where the light still lives. Sunbeams fracture the turquoise shallows and silver schools turn as one. Every dive begins in the glow.

-30m — The Reef Wall

A city that breathes in colour. The reef falls away into blue, dense with coral and quick with fish, before the drop-off swallows the light.

-200m — The Blue

The last light lets go. In the open twilight a vast shadow passes — a whale, unhurried — while marine snow drifts up as you sink.

-1000m — The Midnight Zone

Here the dark makes its own light. Bioluminescent jellies and sparks of plankton burn cold and blue in water that has never seen the sun.

-4000m — The Abyss

The floor of the world, unwitnessed. A vent glows in the black, and something vast moves at the edge of the light. Pressure, silence, and the beginning of everything.
